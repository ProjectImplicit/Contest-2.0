Research Participant Information and Consent Form

Implicit Social Cognition

What you will do in the study: You have been selected for this study because you are an adult of at least 18 years old. You may do some or all of the following: read some instructions, answer some questions, and complete a few tasks such as categorizing words or pictures into groups as fast as possible. After completing this session, you will be contacted again in 24 hours to complete a shorter second session.

Time required: The study will require about 15 minutes for the first session and about 5-10 minutes for the second session. You will receive .5 extra credit points for every half hour of participation or session in this study, rounded up to the nearest half hour. So you will receive .5 points for both sessions of the study.

Risks: While there are no serious risks in the study, some of the tasks may make you feel uncomfortable.

Benefits: There are no direct benefits to you for participating in this research study.

Confidentiality: The information that you give in the study will be shared with collaborators at the University of Virginia who will also be conducting the same study. The information you provide will be handled confidentially. Your information will be assigned a code number. The list connecting your name to this code will be kept in a locked file. When the study is completed and the data have been analyzed, this list will be destroyed. Your name will not be used in any report; only aggregate group data will be used for any reports.

Voluntary participation: Your participation in the study is completely voluntary.

Right to withdraw from the study: You do not have to participate and you have the right to withdraw from the study at any time. You may also choose to skip any questions or tasks that you are not comfortable completing. Not answering questions or withdrawing from the study will have no effect on your grade nor involve any other penalties.
