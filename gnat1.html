The HIT-or-HOLD Game

In the upcoming game, you will see a series of paired words and images. This game involves doing two things at the same time.


1.) For each word/image pair, hit the space bar ONLY IF you see a Black person and Good word together. Do this as quickly as you can.

2.) COUNT the number of times you see a Black person and a Good word together. At the end of game, we will ask you how many times you saw a Black person and a Good word together.

These tasks test your reaction speed and memory and they are difficult! This game is divided into one short practice section and two longer sections.

Click the continue button to begin the game.
